Title: Stay Safe Online – Cyber सुरक्षा Checklist

* Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or banking details with anyone.
* Remember: You do NOT need to enter a PIN to receive money.
* Avoid clicking on unknown or suspicious links sent via SMS, email, or WhatsApp.
* Do not install screen-sharing apps (like AnyDesk) when asked by strangers.
* Verify payment requests carefully before approving any UPI transaction.
* Use only official apps like Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm downloaded from trusted sources.
* Enable app lock and two-factor authentication for all financial apps.
* Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history.
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or financial transactions.
* Immediately report fraud to:
       -National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
       -Helpline: 1930

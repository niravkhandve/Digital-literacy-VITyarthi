I created my digital literacy infographic using Canva. The design explains the meaning and importance of digital literacy for students in a simple and visual way. It includes key topics such as useful digital tools, safe internet practices, and email etiquette. I used a clean layout with icons and short points to make the information easy to understand. One interesting part of this task was designing the layout creatively, while the main challenge was fitting all the information into a single page without making it look crowded. This activity helped me improve my design skills and understand the importance of digital awareness.

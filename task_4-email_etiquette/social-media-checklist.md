# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

## ✅ Do’s

* Think before posting anything online
* Maintain a professional and respectful tone
* Protect your personal information and privacy
* Verify information before sharing
* Use strong passwords and enable security features

## ❌ Don’ts

* Do not share sensitive personal details publicly
* Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
* Do not spread fake news or misinformation
* Avoid engaging in online arguments or trolling
* Do not share your OTPs or passwords with anyone
